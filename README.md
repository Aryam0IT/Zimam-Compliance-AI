Zimam is a bilingual AI-driven platform designed to safeguard Saudi SMEs by ensuring proactive compliance with ZATCA (Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority) regulations.

The project automates the auditing process for financial documents and e-invoices. It uses Amazon Bedrock to perform a "Regulatory Intelligence Check," identifying potential risks like cryptographic mismatches or VAT threshold violations before they result in penalties.

ðŸ’¡ Why the project is useful
-Penalty Prevention: Detects errors such as "Cryptographic Stamp Mismatch" which could lead to heavy fines.
-Real-time Alerts: Notifies users when they approach mandatory registration thresholds (e.g., 375k SAR VAT limit).
-Bilingual Support: Provides actionable insights and legal recommendations in both Arabic and English.
-Smart Auditing: Links every detected risk to the specific ZATCA article for full transparency.

-Access the Portal: Log in through the secure AWS Amplify & Cognito interface.
